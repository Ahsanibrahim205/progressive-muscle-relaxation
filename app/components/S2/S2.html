What is the progressive
muscle relaxation technique?

  • The physician E. Jacobson developed a set of exercises that will enable you to learn effective ways of noticing and then reducing muscular tension wherever it occurs in the body. He called his method ‘progressive relaxation’. The version of Progressive Muscle Relaxation Technique given here is an effective but timeeconomical abbreviation of his overall method and focuses on the 15 main muscle groups of the body.
  • Progressive Muscle Relaxation is a simple active process that reduces the levels of muscular tension in the body quite naturally and automatically. It can also be used to learn and increase your power to distinguish between the contrasting states of muscular relaxation and tension.
